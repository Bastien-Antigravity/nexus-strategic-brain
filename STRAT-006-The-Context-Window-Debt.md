# 👁️ Strategic Audit 006: The Context-Window Debt

## 🔍 Historical Synthesis
The "Documentation-First" policy has successfully populated the Obsidian Brain with thousands of lines of architectural wisdom. However, as noted in `STRAT-004`, we are now reaching a state of **Information Entropy**.

## 🐘 The Elephant in the Room (Blind Spot)
We are currently paying a **"Fragmentation Tax"** on every AI interaction.

*   **The Problem**: The brain is split into 10+ repositories. To answer a simple question about "How to log a market event," the AI might need to pull context from `business-bdd`, `tech-stack`, and `nexus-strategic`.
*   **The Risk**: AI agents start "Hallucinating-by-Volume." When the context window is filled with 80% governance logs and only 20% relevant code logic, the quality of reasoning degrades.

## ⚖️ Global Law Assessment: "The SNR Crisis"
The Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR) in the Brain is currently **Decreasing**. We are generating documentation faster than we can synthesize it.

## 🎯 Oracle's Recommendation
1.  **Immediate Brain Consolidation**: Execute the merge of `03-Tech-Stack` and `07-Core-KMS` into a singular **03-Engineering-Brain**.
2.  **Log Distillation**: Activate the **Knowledge Compression Script** (`20-Scripts/compress_brain.py` or similar). Convert raw session logs into high-density "Pattern Cards."
3.  **Milestone Sync Only**: End the "Mandatory Session Log" for minor tweaks. Only sync to the brain when a "Mission Sign-off" is achieved.
